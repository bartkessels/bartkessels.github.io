The broccoli is a vegetable I really enjoy eating without any other flavours or spices required. When you simply steam or boil the broccoli it already has a fantastic taste. The only thing better is having your own grown broccoli!

This schedule assumes that you have a proper environment indoors for growing up the seedlings. In the Netherlands, were I'm doing this, I require a heating mat and a grow light because the temperature isn't at point and the sun hours are extremely short.

## Schedule

The schedule I'm following right now, is purely based on my little bit of experience with growing broccoli and the information on the packaging of the seeds.

Right now I'm also experimenting with using a heat mat and a growing light, which I'm not sure if it's required. But I don't think it'll do any harm.
