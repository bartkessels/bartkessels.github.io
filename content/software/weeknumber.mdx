WeekNumber is a minimalist Android application that does one thing well: showing you the current week number.

## Features

- **Widget Support**: Home screen widget for quick access
- **Multiple Formats**: ISO 8601 and US week numbering
- **Privacy First**: No analytics, no tracking, no permissions needed
- **Material You**: Dynamic theming on Android 12+

## Why?

Sometimes you just need to know what week it is. No frills, no subscriptions, no ads—just the week number.
